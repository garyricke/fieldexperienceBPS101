The Case for Change

Why Change? — The Students

  • The world of work is changing — AI and automation are reshaping industries faster than curricula can keep up
  • College is too expensive to get wrong — students can't afford career changes after investing years and tuition
  • Let them try before they commit — a student who "loves engineering" should find out at 17, not 22

"Try now to see if it's the right fit."

The Course

Field Experience — Redesigned

  • Replacing the 20-year-old Link course with a modern WBL program
  • Boot Camp (2–3 weeks) — employability skills, professional expectations, workplace readiness
  • Then placement into one of four experience types
  • Aligned with ISBE's 91-page WBL manual and all CCPE requirements
Experience Types

Four Pathways, One Course

01 — Student-Led

Incubator entrepreneurs continuing their own business ventures

02 — School-Based

School store, BHS merchandise, Bulldog Media Team

03 — Clinical

Medically focused placements through BNAT and healthcare partners

04 — Internships

60+ hours with community employers — the cornerstone of CCPE

All four types begin with the Boot Camp foundation.
