Connect Your Google Sheet

This page reads your Google Form responses directly from your Sheet via its published CSV URL. One-time setup — saves automatically.

  1. 1. Open your Google Sheet (the one collecting form responses)
  2. 2. File → Share → Publish to web
  3. 3. Under "Link", select the sheet tab and choose Comma-separated values (.csv)
  4. 4. Click Publish and copy the URL
  5. 5. Paste it below
